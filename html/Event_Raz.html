Radoz's Raz In order to continue its work and activities while serving Kurdistan, Radoz Organisation is organising a charity campaign known as Raz Event during the holy month of Ramadan. The campaign will be held in Erbil and Sulaimani with speakers and celebrities participating. These events, which are free and open to the public at no cost, help to increase the impact of the campaign by providing a variety of activities that benefit individuals and young people, especially during the month of Ramadan. Event revenue, which comes from sponsors or public contributions, goes directly to funds that help poor families and orphans..
